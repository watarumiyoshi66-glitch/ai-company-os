# AI Company OS — Claude Instructions

## What This Repo Is

This is the operating system for an AI-native solo company run by a global enterprise IT sales professional.
Everything here — content, agents, workflows, research — compounds over time into a business asset.

---

## Folder Roles

| Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `/company` | Business strategy, positioning, OKRs |
| `/agents` | AI agent definitions, personas, system prompts |
| `/content` | X posts, Note drafts, content calendar |
| `/memory` | Reusable knowledge: sales, AI, market insights |
| `/workflows` | Step-by-step SOPs for recurring tasks |
| `/research` | Raw and synthesized research on AI/enterprise topics |
| `/reports` | Weekly reviews, KPI logs |

---

## Operating Rules

- **Never publish automatically** — always ask before any external post
- **Read before write** — check existing files before creating new ones
- **Compound knowledge** — every useful insight goes into `/memory`
- **Japanese-first** — default output language is Japanese unless specified
- **Concise and strategic** — executive-level clarity, no filler

---

## Content Style

- Tone: strategic, practical, insightful
- Audience: enterprise IT buyers, business leaders, AI-curious professionals
- Format: short paragraphs, clear structure, action-oriented

---

## Focus Areas

- AI Agents & Orchestration
- Enterprise AI (SAP, Salesforce, ServiceNow, etc.)
- SaaS / GTM strategy
- Executive communication
- Productivity and automation
- Future of work

## What Claude Should Do Here

1. **Draft content** — X posts and Note articles aligned to focus areas
2. **Build workflows** — capture repeatable processes in `/workflows`
3. **Store knowledge** — distill key insights into `/memory`
4. **Define agents** — write clear personas and prompts in `/agents`
5. **Synthesize research** — turn raw sources into usable intelligence in `/research`
6. **Review weekly** — help produce weekly reports in `/reports`
